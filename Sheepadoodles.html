About Sheepadoodles

Sheepaddodles are a cross-breed between Old English Sheep Dogs and Poodles.
Their name was coined in 1992

Sheepadoodles hava a lifespan of 12 - 15 years. They are very smart and easy to train.

Hair

You don't need to worry about hair, because they shed very lightly.
Their coat is long, soft, and curly.

Traits

  • 22-38 kg (50-85 lbs)
  • 45-58 cm (18-23 in)
  • 18–27 inches tall
